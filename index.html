Buy WBTC FAQ

Where should I buy WBTC?

Start from the official WBTC domain to verify resources, then use a supported merchant, exchange, wallet, or DEX route appropriate for your jurisdiction and custody preferences.

Is WBTC the same as BTC?

No. WBTC is a wrapped token that represents Bitcoin in Ethereum-compatible apps, while BTC remains the native Bitcoin asset.

Which WBTC contract should I check?

On Ethereum, check 0x2260fac5e5542a773aa44fbcfedf7c193bc2c599 before accepting quotes or deposits.

Do I need ETH to move WBTC?

On Ethereum mainnet, WBTC transfers and swaps require ETH for gas; on other supported networks, use that network's native gas token.

Can WBTC be redeemed for Bitcoin?

Redemption depends on the merchant, exchange, or custodian route you use, so review KYC requirements, fees, timing, and network support first.
